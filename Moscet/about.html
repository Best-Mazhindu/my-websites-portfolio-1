COMPANY OVERVIEW

Energize Society Reliable Energy

Mos-Sun Clean Energy Technologies (PTY) Ltd, trading as MOSCET, is a leading renewable energy technology company based in Lesotho. Since our establishment in 2010, we have been committed to revolutionizing the energy landscape and making a positive impact on communities across the Kingdom of Lesotho.

At MOSCET, we are dedicated to our key missions, which include bridging the energy gap by providing electricity access to underserved regions, fostering affordability by reducing electricity bills, and advancing as an independent power producer. Our fundamental goal is to offer clean, efficient, and reliable energy solutions that empower thousands of households, communities, institutions, small and large businesses. We firmly believe that sustainable energy not only enhances the quality of life and drives economic growth but also safeguards our environment for future generations.

As part of our vision to advance as an Independent Power Producer, we have established the remarkable MOSCET Wind Farm feasibility study in Semonkong, Lesotho. With a capacity of 35MW, this wind farm will represents our commitment to clean energy generation and our pivotal role as an independent and reliable power producer. By expanding our portfolio and exploring further opportunities, we are poised to make significant strides in the renewable energy sector.

Through these initiatives, MOSCET aims to transform the energy landscape by ensuring access to electricity, promoting affordability, and spearheading sustainable energy production. By embracing renewable resources and partnering with local communities, we are dedicated to driving positive change and shaping a brighter and greener future for all.
