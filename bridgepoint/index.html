Let’s Meet with Our Director

MUNYARADZI HILARY MUDARIKI

Our director
MUNYARADZI HILARY MUDARIKI

A seasoned and highly motivated senior business development/investment executive with a successful track record in promoting, facilitating foreign direct investment and advising businesses. Recognized for exceptional problem solving and project management skills as well as the ability to promote, negotiate, facilitate and close deals successfully across cultural barriers.

He worked at the Zimbabwe Investment Authority, a government organization responsible for promoting and facilitating foreign direct investment into the country for 13 years and left as a Senior Executive. Since 2006 he has been offering independent consultancy through Hilfar Capital Investments Pty Ltd not Pvt Ltd, a company he established in 2006. He has also been promoting and facilitating Foreign Direct investment projects in Zimbabwe, South Africa. Namibia. Zambia and Mozambique and advising investors on political trends in Africa to enable them to make informed decisions.

He is a graduate of the University of Zimbabwe and holds three university degrees in economic history and English including an MSc in Tourism. He been trained in investment promotion, facilitation and project management in a number of countries including Australia, Malaysia, Taiwan, China and South Africa

Why we exist

When starting up in a foreign country it is often difficult to understand the business terrain. Through our expertise, commitment and experience we are therefore the bridge between businesses and the countries they desire to locate in Southern Africa and the world at large. Hilfar Capital Investments Pty Ltd not Pvt Ltd focuses on its partners and the host countries and endeavors to create win –win opportunities for all. We offer a range of services and add value to companies big or small ranging from providing information project research, identifying the opportunities, writing the business plan, facilitating the project approval process to project implementation guidance

Extensive experience in Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) consulting

We have extensive experience in Foreign Direct Investment ( FDI) consulting and business development in diverse sectors ranging from manufacturing, agri-business, finance, mining, education, services, international development agencies, infrastructure and travel/tourism and conservation.
