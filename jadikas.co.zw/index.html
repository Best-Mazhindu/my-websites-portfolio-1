APPROVALS, COMPLIANCES AND REGULATORY ISSUES

-Permit to subdivide the land was obtained from the
Department of Physical Planning before commencement of works
- An environmental impact assessment of the project was done and submitted to the
Environmental Management Agency (EMA),who gave their approval through the issuance
of an EIA Certificate
- Engineering drawings were submitted and approved by the City of Harare Council.
It is against this that a compliance certificate will be obtained upon completion of
all works. This certificate is also used in the transfer of title deeds to individual
purchasers. - Cadastrial survey of the project area has been finalized and the general plans have
