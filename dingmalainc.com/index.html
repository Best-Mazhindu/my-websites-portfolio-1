Who We Are

Dingmala is wholly owned and proudly Zimbabwean company with its focus in the mining ad resort towns of Hwange and victoria falls respectively though not limited to towns and cities in and around Zimbabwe. The name of the company was derived from the director's last names. Dingmala is into procurement, logistics, import and export as well as supply of coporate wares and events management.this company is directly owned and engineered by women with a passion to empower fellow women, youths and its surrounding communities at large.
