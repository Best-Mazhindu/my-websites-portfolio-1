ABOUT BFC

Why Most People Choose
Our Services

BFC Consultancy was established in March 2009, with the sole purpose of providing marketing and business management consultancy services to corporates, private companies and SMEs in the areas of marketing, research, management, training, entrepreneurship, sales and business start-ups. BFC Consultancy has developed a fine-tuned and successful synergy between innovative consultancy solutions and a solid theoretical base, resulting in new dimensions in consultancy and training services. BFC Consultancy is proud of its strong client focus and long-term relationships. Our aim is to work with our clients, embracing their organizational culture and vision in order to come up with relevant consultancy services that offer real value for money. Attention to detail and quality of work, coupled with experience of our consultancy specialits, make us the perfect choice to partner with to unlock hidden human and organizational potential.
