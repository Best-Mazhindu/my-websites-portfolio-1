About Our Parent Company

CANK Electrical Engineers Pvt Ltd t/a Cank Electrical and Electronic Engineers has been providing electronic engineering services to the Zimbabwean community since 2014. We are made up of young vibrant engineers dedicated to provide quality services in competitive, positive, close controlled and professional manner. As CANK, we are so proficient in the execution of projects.

Proficient In The Execution

Our field of expertise covers all types of electrical and electronic building services in projects as diverse as showrooms, banks, commercial and retail complexes, service stations, housing, sporting facilities and many more.
