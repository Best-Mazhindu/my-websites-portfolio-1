What Is Kingpin ?

Kingpin is a pivot pin that links you to an insurer of quality at affordable rates. Kingpin ensures a seamless claims process in the event of a loss and provides you with peace of mind never before seen.Kingpin Insurance holds its social responsibility as a core value and firmly believes in transparency and Education of customers as an ethical requirement. This is whereby clients are empowered to make faster and better decisions about their own unique insurance and risk management requirements.

Our Mission

To serve individuals, corporate entities and public sector institutions by offering cost effective solutions to the physical and pecuniary risks they are confronted with and delighting them on all occasions.

Our Vision

Becoming the best front line Insurer with Excellence in Zimbabwe. Kingpin Insurance Agency’s devotion is to combine skills and professionalism in management, underwriting, marketing and the introduction of innovative policies in the provision of excellent insurance service.
