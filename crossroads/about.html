A Financial services company

Crossroad Financial Services (CFS) was established as a Financial services company to provide financial services to the unbanked in Zimbabwe. The company was formed from the realisation of the need to assist all the unemployed and also ensuring that the people in the rural Zimbabwe are provided with services in order for them to build capacity.

CFS provides loans to SME in all the main sectors of the economy. CFS offers real opportunity to establish or develop businesses faster and more cost effectively with reduced capital outlay.

CFS is a registered Microfinance company in terms of the Companies Act [Chapter 24:03] registration number 4317/2010. It is regulated by the Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe and governed by the Microfinance Act [Chapter 24:29]. AFS got its lending license in April of 2014 however opened its doors to the public on the April 2015.
