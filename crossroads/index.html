Registration and Licensing

CFS opened its doors to the public on the 1st of April 2015, following licensing by the Registrar of Companies and the Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe.

CFS was licenced as a Microfinance Institution in Zimbabwe by the Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe on 15 January 2015. As a Microfinance Institution, CFS will make a greater impact on asset finance for SMEs. Further, the developmental impact will strengthen.

Under the Lending Licence, CFS is permitted to offer the following services:
  • a) Provision of credit facilities.
  • b) Provision of financial payment services such as ZESA, ecocash etc.
  • c) Provision of leasing facilities for SME.
  • d) Provision of housing microfinance.
